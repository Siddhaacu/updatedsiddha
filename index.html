About Siddha Saraswatha

Siddha Saraswatha offers authentic Vedic astrology consultations — muhurtha selection, panchangam guidance, remedies, and personalised readings to help you with career, health, relationships and spiritual growth.

Led by R.B.S. Goutham Sastri (Jyotish Shastri), our practice blends classical Vedic techniques with practical remedies and clear guidance tailored to modern life.

With over a decade of experience, R.B.S. Goutham Sastri specialises in naming muhurthas (Namakarana), Annaprashana, Griha Pravesa, marriage muhurthas, career & finance readings, and health consultations. Each reading includes a precise panchangam, remedial suggestions (mantras, homas, yantras, gemstones), and a follow-up plan.

We conduct consultations via phone, video or in-person. Our goal is to give you actionable insights — not vague predictions. For important ceremonies (weddings, house-entry, naming), we provide exact muhurtha windows backed by traditional calculations and modern timing tools.

Book a session to receive a detailed Kundali analysis and customised remedies. We respect privacy, and all readings are handled with confidentiality.
