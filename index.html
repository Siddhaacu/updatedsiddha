Dr. D. Vijayanandanatha (Guruji)

Dr. D. Vijayanandanatha, also known as Guruji, is a distinguished scholar and spiritual guide, holding titles like Sahithya Vidya Vachaspati, Sahitya Vidyalankara, and Acharya. He is the founder of Ravindra Bharathi Gurukula Vidyalaya, established in the early 1990s. Guruji is a devoted disciple of Srimad Paramahamsa Parivrajaka Acharya Avadhuta Sri Chandrasekharendra Saraswathi Swamy, the former Peetadhipathi of Sri Kanchi Peetam, Kanchipuram.
