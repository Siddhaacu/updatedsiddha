About Siddha Saraswatha

Led by R.B.S. Goutham Sastri (Jyotish Shastri), we provide authentic Vedic astrology consultations — muhurtha, panchangam, remedies and personalised guidance for modern life.

We specialise in naming muhurthas (Namakarana), Annaprashana, Griha Pravesa, marriage muhurthas, career & finance readings, and health consultations.

Each reading includes a precise panchangam, remedial suggestions (mantras, homas, gemstones), and a follow-up plan. Consultations are available by phone, video or in-person. Our goal is to provide actionable insights, not vague predictions.

For important ceremonies we provide exact muhurtha windows using traditional calculations. We respect your privacy — all readings are confidential.
