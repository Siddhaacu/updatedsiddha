Our Temple

Asta Dasha Bhuji Maha Lakshmi is a vibrant and active congregation that provides a religious, social, and educational framework for the local Hindu community. Established in 1997, it was founded on the ideals of inclusivity, offering everyone a chance to learn, grow, and define their own understanding of Hinduism.

Contact us today to discover how you can become a part of our welcoming and spiritually enriching community.

Asta bhuji Durga Varnana

ఖడ్గం చక్ర గదేషుచాప పరిఘా శూలం భుశుండీం శిరః
శంంఖం సందధతీం కరైస్త్రినయనాం సర్వాంగభూషావృతామ్ ।
యాం హంతుం మధుకైభౌ జలజభూస్తుష్టావ సుప్తే హరౌ
నీలాశ్మద్యుతి మాస్యపాదదశకాం సేవే మహాకాళికాం॥

Khadgam Chakra Gadeshuchapa Parigha Shulam Bhusundeem Shirah

Nilashamdyuti Masyapadadasakam Seva Mahakalikam ||

Maha Saraswathi Varnana

ఘంటాశూలహలాని శంఖ ముసలే చక్రం ధనుః సాయకం
హస్తాబ్జైర్ధదతీం ఘనాంతవిలసచ్ఛీతాంశుతుల్యప్రభాం ।
గౌరీ దేహ సముద్భవాం త్రిజగతాం ఆధారభూతాం మహా
పూర్వామత్ర సరస్వతీ మనుభజే శుంభాదిదైత్యార్దినీం॥

Chair Person

The Chairperson of the Temple plays a pivotal role in leading and managing the activities of the temple, ensuring it serves as a spiritual, cultural, and educational hub for the community. Responsible for overseeing operations, coordinating events, and fostering inclusivity, the Chairperson works closely with members, volunteers, and devotees to uphold the temple's mission and values. Their leadership helps maintain a welcoming environment and supports the growth and vibrancy of the congregation.
