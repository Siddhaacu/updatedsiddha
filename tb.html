Tarabalam

Strength of constellations used for Muhurtham, travel, marriage and major activities.

Overview Method Types Chandrabalam Details

Overview

Tarabalam is used for evaluating auspiciousness for travel, marriage, business, and major undertakings.

Matrix of Calculation

Count from your Janma Nakshatra to the day's Nakshatra (inclusive). Divide by 9. The remainder determines the Tara:
