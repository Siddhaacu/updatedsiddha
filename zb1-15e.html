viniyōgaḥ
mātā marakataśyāmā mātaṅgī madaśālinī ।
kuryātkaṭākṣaṃ kaḻyāṇī kadambavanavāsinī ॥ 3 ॥

stuti
jaya mātaṅgatanayē jaya nīlōtpaladyutē ।
jaya saṅgītarasikē jaya līlāśukapriyē ॥ 4 ॥
