Our Purpose & Journey

At Siddha Saraswatha we empower individuals through personalized astrological insights. Our journey began in 2020, driven by a commitment to make Vedic astrology accurate, approachable and practical for modern life.

What we do

We provide muhurtha selection, panchangam guidance, remedial suggestions (mantras, homas, gemstones) and detailed readings for career, health, relationships and spiritual growth. Consultations are available by phone, video and in-person.

Dr. D. Vijayanandanatha (Guruji)

Dr. D. Vijayanandanatha is a distinguished scholar and spiritual guide — founder of Ravindra Bharathi Gurukula Vidyalaya. He is a disciple of Sri Chandrasekharendra Saraswathi Swamy and has established temples such as the Asta Dasha Bhuji Maha Lakshmi Temple in Hasthinapuram.

With blessings from Rajarajeshwari Peetham, Guruji also founded Astro9s.com to extend astrological guidance to a broader audience.
