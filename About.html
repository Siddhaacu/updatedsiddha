Spiritual Contributions

Guruji has established several temples, including the renowned Asta Dasha Bhuji Maha Lakshmi Temple in Hasthinapuram, Hyderabad. Inspired by the Saptashati Chandi, the temple honors Goddess Chandi Matha with 18 hands and 18 symbolic weapons, representing the victory over negativity and the cultivation of a positive aura for devotees.


Founder of Astro9s.com

The platform extends astrology-based guidance to help individuals overcome difficulties and achieve harmony.
