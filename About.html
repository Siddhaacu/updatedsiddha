About Siddha Saraswatha

Discover how Siddha Saraswatha combines ancient Vedic astrology with practical remedies and modern tools to deliver personalised guidance.

Our Purpose & Journey

At Siddha Saraswatha we empower individuals through personalised astrological insights. Our journey began in 2020, driven by a commitment to make Vedic astrology accurate, approachable and practical for modern life.

Spiritual Contributions

Guruji has established several temples, including the Asta Dasha Bhuji Maha Lakshmi Temple in Hasthinapuram, Hyderabad. Inspired by Saptashati Chandi, the temple honors Goddess Chandi Matha and supports spiritual practice among devotees.

Founder of Astro9s.com

With blessings from Rajarajeshwari Peetham and deep knowledge in Indian astrology, Guruji founded Astro9s.com to make astrological guidance accessible and practical for a wider audience.

Mission & Vision

We aim to share Indian astrological wisdom with integrity—empowering people with clear guidance and remedies that support positive transformation.

Dr. D. Vijayanandanatha (Guruji)

Dr. D. Vijayanandanatha is a distinguished scholar and spiritual guide — founder of Ravindra Bharathi Gurukula Vidyalaya and a disciple of Sri Chandrasekharendra Saraswathi Swamy. He continues to provide spiritual leadership and practical guidance to devotees.

Guruji established the Asta Dasha Bhuji Maha Lakshmi Temple and continues community and spiritual work under the auspices of Rajarajeshwari Peetham.
