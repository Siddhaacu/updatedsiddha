Dr. D. Vijayanandanatha (Guruji)

Dr. D. Vijayanandanatha, also known as Guruji, is a distinguished scholar and spiritual guide, holding titles like Sahithya Vidya Vachaspati, Sahitya Vidyalankara, and Acharya. He is the founder of Ravindra Bharathi Gurukula Vidyalaya, established in the early 1990s. Guruji is a devoted disciple of Srimad Paramahamsa Parivrajaka Acharya Avadhuta Sri Chandrasekharendra Saraswathi Swamy, the former Peetadhipathi of Sri Kanchi Peetam, Kanchipuram.


Spiritual Contributions

Guruji has established several temples, including the renowned Asta Dasha Bhuji Maha Lakshmi Temple in Hasthinapuram, Hyderabad. Inspired by the Saptashati Chandi, the temple honors Goddess Chandi Matha with 18 hands and 18 symbolic weapons, representing the victory over negativity and the cultivation of a positive aura for devotees.


Founder of Astro9s.com

With divine blessings from Rajarajeshwari Peetham and profound knowledge in Indian astrology and Siddha Saraswatha traditions, Guruji created the online platform Astro9s.com to provide solutions for life’s challenges. The platform extends astrology-based guidance to help individuals overcome difficulties and achieve harmony.

Rajarajeshwari Peetham

Under the auspices of Rajarajeshwari Peetham, Guruji has served humanity for over a Many decades. The Peetham offers free consultations addressing various life issues, such as health concerns, family disputes, and business challenges. Guruji provides divine remedies and spiritual support, fostering hope and healing among countless devotees.


Mission and Vision

Guruji’s work is driven by the mission to share the wisdom of Indian astrology and spirituality for the benefit of humanity. With the blessings of the Almighty Jagat-janani, the focus is on meaningful and practical solutions that bring positive transformation to people’s lives.
